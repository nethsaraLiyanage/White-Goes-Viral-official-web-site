161718 Mahindians

2016, 2017, 2018 are three independent groups, whose members have recently left the school, affiliated with the Old Boys Association of Mahinda College. These three groups have joined hands with one common vision and one common aim. The reasoning behind the merger was that since there was a certain synergy between the thought processes of the members of each group, working as a single group would enable more expansive projects to be undertaken far more easily. The l 6l 7l 8Mahindians collaboration has developed into such a state of unity and strength that the existence of the independent 2016, 2017, 2018 groups are hardly ever mentioned.



161718 Mahindians - Vision

To create a generation of Mahindians with skills, knowledge, attitude and the capacity to face the growing challenges of the 21st Century.

161718 Mahindians - Mission

To help make Mahinda College Galle the premier school in South Asia in the fields of education, global technologies, new and upcoming trends and in future careers within the new decade in order to make a Mahindan with the attitude & aptitude to face the growing challenges of the 21st century.

'16 Group

2016 batch is a group with an appraisable potential who supported many events linked to Mahinda Richmond Annual Big match. We established a good name as 2016 batch by joining hands with other groups of the MCOBA in numerous projects. We are looking forward to broaden our efforts in ensuring the wellbeing of the school and its students by organizing fund raising projects. The sole aim of the group is to uplift the school by taking necessary actions to develop it in terms of its physical values as well as to help our alma mater to generate well-balanced personalities as we have done for the past 127 years.

'17 Group

'17 Batch is a registered group of Mahinda College Old Boys' Association. 17 Batch is a group where all its decisions are taken upon the majority vote of the members and all in the betterment of members and school. '17 Group hopes to become a beacon of hope and encouragement to the generations to come. The '17 group has a vision of getting all of them together & developing a friendly environment where all of the students can contribute for the betterment of the school. Even after leaving school the '17 batch has been collaborating with the senior batches to organize some major projects. And will continue in supporting the school and uplifting the school name by taking necessary actions. The constitution of the batch governs the functionality of the group and lays down all the rules and regulations. The Primary motive in creating this group is to ensure that all the members of 2017 batch will get connected and start working together as a single unit in achieving their goals.

'18 Group

'18 batch is the youngest member of the Old Boys' Association of the school and is a registered group. By creating and strengthening connections between Mahinda College and its alumni, and by reflecting and leveraging their commitment to each other, '18 batch will help Mahinda College to realize its goal of being a clear leader in the social and academic community. The members have the experience of conducting successful events together with senior batches on their events. '18 batch would help the future generations of the college to reach the dynamic standards of the competitive community. Furthermore, they would ensure that they help to build a united community. They have conducted several community projects to uplift the living standards of masses in the community. With members moving on to careers, the batch hopes to actively contribute more in the future.
